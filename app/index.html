What is Tanuki Gaming?

Games Library

We’ve put togather a curated library so you can come and play games right here. Try before you buy or get some friends together and explore modern boardgames in a great setting with craft beer and Yakitori.

Games Shop

We sell a great selection of modern boardgames - at great prices. From the newest, hottest releases to the recent classics, we try to have something to suit all tastes. Come and check out what we have in-stock or check out our catalogue online. Pop in and browse.

Events

Covid permitting, we will run regular events with community gaming groups and other pop-up events. If you have a group and need a place to to play - please contact us for some great deals!

Food and Drink

Gaming is hungy and thirsty work. Our friends at Badger Badger provide awesome craft beers, wines and softdrinks. They also do great Japanese Yakitori street food.

How is Tanuki?

Gaming Library

Wednesday

Every Wednesday we host and run our own meet-up group at Tanuki. Sign-up and join us. Perfect if you are looking to make new friends and meet new people through gaming. Everyone is welcome - no experience necessary.
