What is Tanuki?

Games Library

We’ve put togather a curated library so you can come and play games right here. Try before you buy or get some friends together and explore modern boardgames in a great setting with craft beer and Yakitori.

Games Shop

We sell a great selection of modern boardgames - at great prices. From the newest, hottest releases to the recent classics, we try to have something to suit all tastes. Come and check out what we have in-stock or check out our catalogue online. Order online and pick-up or just pop in and browse.

Events

Covid permitting, we run regular events with community gaming groups and other special pop-up events. If you have a group and need a place to to play - please contact us for some great deals! Check out our events section for more details.

Food and Drink

Gaming is hungy and thirsty work. Our friends at Badger Badger provide awesome craft beers, softdrinks, coffee and teas. They also do great Japanese Yakitori street food. What can be better than Yakitori, beer and the best games around.

Who is Tanuki?

We are a brand new board game pub in Deptford, South London, 5-minutes from London Bridge.

We’re Dom and Alan. We've been running a boardgaming group in Walthamstow, North East London since 2015.

The Walthamstow Boardgames Group started small and just kept growing and is now a fantastic community of all types of wonderful people and dogs (if you're in that area you should really check us out)

Through that group we met the great guys at Badger Badger and together we decided to build an equally beautiful gaming community with a new pop-up gaming pub, shop and library in Deptford.
