What is Tanuki Gaming?

Games Library

We’ve put together a curated boardgame library so you can come and play games right here. Try before you buy or get some friends together and explore modern boardgames in a great setting with craft beer and Yakitori. Book a table - prices are £5 per person for a session.

Games Shop

We sell a great selection of modern boardgames, from the newest, hottest releases to the recent classics, we try to have something to suit all tastes. Come and check out what we have in stock. We will soon be adding our catalogue online. Pop in and browse, we have great prices and will be offering membership deals in the coming months.

Events

Covid permitting, we will run regular events with community gaming groups and other pop-up events. If you have a group and need a place to to play - please contact us for some great deals!

Community

Tanuki Gaming is the brainchild of Dom and Alan. Our roots are from North East London, in Walthamstow. We have been running a board game club in East London since 2015 and through that club a great community of gamers has developed. We succeeded in building a friendly and inclusive group of several hundred people and we hope we can do the same in Deptford over the coming years.

We are a brand new board game site in Deptford, South London, 10-minutes from London Bridge.

Gaming Library

Stay and Play

We have something for everyone in the library. From modern classics to the best new releases, we've got your gaming needs covered. From chasing zombies, to trick-taking card games. From party and word games to heavy Euro strategy games - we have it all. Ask us for advice if you are not sure what to play. We will do our best to recommend a game to suit your time and interest. Sessions currently are limited to 3 hours but if you have a specific game you want to play that will run longer, please let us know.

Covid

Guidelines

These are unique times. We are taking every precaution to ensure that all our customers have a safe experience. We are following government guidelines and implementing extra measures. We provide anti-bacterial gel and gloves and we have well spaced-out tables to ensure you can safely play with your own household bubble. We also have a limit of 2-people at most at the library. We can deliver games to the table and provide you with lists of games so you don't need to leave your seat.

Yakitori

Gaming is hungy and thirsty work. Our friends at Badger Badger provide awesome craft beers, wines and refreshments. They also do great Japanese street food.
